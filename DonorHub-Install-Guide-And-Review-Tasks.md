# DonorHub Alpha - Install Guide & Review Tasks

This document has two parts: a short **install guide** and **review tasks** to prepare for your CTO discussion.

---

## Part 1: Install Guide

### Requirements

- **Node.js** 18 or later (or latest LTS)
- **npm** (included with Node.js)

### Steps

1. **Get the DonorHub project** on your computer (clone or download and unzip) into a folder, e.g. `DonorHub` or `DonorHub-Alpha`.

2. **Open a terminal** (Command Prompt, PowerShell, or Terminal) and go to the project folder:
   ```text
   cd path/to/DonorHub
   ```
   Use your actual path (e.g. `cd C:\Users\YourName\Documents\DonorHub` or `cd ~/Documents/DonorHub`).

3. **Install dependencies:**
   ```text
   npm run install:all
   ```
   Wait for it to finish (about 1–3 minutes).

4. **Start DonorHub:**
   ```text
   npm start
   ```
   Leave this terminal window open.

5. **Open the app in your browser:**  
   Go to **http://localhost:3000**

6. **Stop the app when done:**  
   Press **Ctrl+C** in the terminal.

For more detail (Node.js install, troubleshooting), see the main **README.md** in the DonorHub project.

## Part 2: Review Tasks

Please complete these tasks as you explore DonorHub. Use your answers in your CTO presentation (about 10–15 minutes).

---

### Task 1: First impression (about 5 min)

After using the platform for a few minutes:

- What is your overall impression?
- What felt intuitive? What felt confusing?

---

### Task 2: Dashboard & metrics

- Are the KPIs shown relevant for a fundraising manager?
- What would you add to the dashboard for daily use?

---

### Task 3: Campaign management

- How well does the campaign flow match how you plan and run campaigns?
- What would make it more useful for your workflow?

---

### Task 4: Donor management

- Are donor details and segments sufficient for your work?
- How would you use donor segments in real campaigns?

---

### Task 5: Reports & communications

- Do the reports give you what you need for stakeholders or boards?
- How would you improve the communications or thank-you flow?

---

### Task 6: Missing features

- List the **top 3 features** you would add for an NGO like Germanwatch (or similar).
- Explain why each matters.

---

### Task 7: Real-world fit

- Could you see DonorHub replacing or complementing tools you use today?
- What would hold you back from adopting it?

---

### Presentation format

- **Length:** 10–15 minutes  
- **Format:** Share screen, walk through DonorHub, present your answers  
- **Include:** 2–3 concrete recommendations for improvement  

After the presentation, the CTO can answer technical questions and discuss next steps.
